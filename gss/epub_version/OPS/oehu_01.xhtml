Introduction

Young carpenter Damien flunked out of community college, so he fumbles and struggles to understand why the worlds of business and politics are upsidedown for himself, his fiancé, and for his generation.  A wild young woman named Deshajoins his fight.  In secret they meet two wise mentors who reveal the hidden path to flipping the world right-side up.  In the wild conclusion their worlds collide in surprising ways.

Are you angry at the craziness going on in U.S. politics?  Are you frustrated that the United States Congress lacks a backbone and couldn't crawl it's way out of an open paper bag lying on its side?  Do you fear economic hard times, too many pandemic deaths, more school shootings, and overpriced access to kitten videos?

This is the entertaining comic story of a young man named Damien and a young woman named Desha who together dig under many layers of political dung and entertaining distractions to uncover the secret tactic that wealthy business owners use to control politicians in both the Republican and Democratic parties.

Even better, Damien and Desha learn the solution that eventually will reduce corruption in politics.  The name of the solution is simple.  It's called pairwise vote counting.  What does this mean?  Read the story to join Damien and Desha in their insightful adventure!
